more than marketing

Est. 2025

We're two girls — one with a love for branding and storytelling, the other with a talent for numbers and strategy — who decided to put our passions together.

At MTM (More Than Marketing), we believe every small business deserves to look big.

We're a micro-agency that combines creativity with financial expertise to help brands grow smart and strong.

From logos and content to budgeting and campaigns, we bring both sides of marketing together: the creative and the strategic.

With us, you get balance, personal touch, and solutions built just for you.

Mission

To empower small businesses with accessible, high-quality branding and marketing solutions.

Vision

To become the go-to creative partner for Egypt's emerging brands.
